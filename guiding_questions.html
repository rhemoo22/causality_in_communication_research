GUIDING QUESTIONS
  • How, if at all, can we use causal inference to develop new theories and sunset older ones?
  • Should we consider ‘relaxing’ key requirements of causal inference when studying and theorizing about communication in the real-world?
  • What kind of data is needed to make causal inferences across levels of analysis (e.g., individuals, teams, communities, societies)?
  • What is the role of causal inference in qualitatively-driven inquiry and community-based participatory research?
